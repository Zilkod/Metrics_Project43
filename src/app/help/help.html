Welcome to Linear

We're thrilled to have you with us. Linear will help you achieve pixel-perfect designs without getting in your way.

In Linear, you work with rulers. Here is one. Go ahead, move it around :-)

To manage your rulers, have a look at the icon in the menu bar, located at the top right corner of your screen. From there, you can create new rulers. You can create as many rulers as you want.

Shortcuts are really useful! To create a new ruler, press ^⌘R. To hide/show (toggle) existing rulers, press ^⌘T. To close a ruler, press ⌘W. To toggle the center guides, press ⌘;

Right-clicking on a ruler lets you duplicate it. The duplicated ruler keeps the original ruler's size and general position.

In the settings window (available from clicking the icon in the menu bar) you can change the displayed ruler's unit to either px or em.
